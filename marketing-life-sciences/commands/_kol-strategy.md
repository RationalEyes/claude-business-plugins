# KOL Strategy

<!-- Life Sciences Addition -->

> If you see unfamiliar placeholders or need to check which tools are connected, see [CONNECTORS.md](../CONNECTORS.md).

Develop a comprehensive Key Opinion Leader (KOL) engagement strategy for a therapeutic area or product, including KOL identification, tiering, engagement planning, and measurement.

## Trigger

User runs `/kol-strategy` or asks to develop a KOL engagement plan, map opinion leaders, build a KOL strategy, identify thought leaders in a therapeutic area, or plan advisory board composition.

## Inputs

Gather the following from the user. If not provided, ask before proceeding:

1. **Therapeutic area or product** — the disease area or specific product for which KOL engagement is needed (e.g., "non-small cell lung cancer", "KEYTRUDA", "type 2 diabetes")

2. **Strategic objective** — why KOL engagement is needed:
   - Pre-launch market shaping and disease education
   - Launch support (speaker programs, advisory boards, clinical data dissemination)
   - Post-launch evidence generation and lifecycle management
   - Competitive defense or repositioning
   - Pipeline development (clinical trial design, investigator recruitment)

3. **Geography** — target market(s) for KOL engagement:
   - US national
   - US regional (specify regions)
   - EU (specify countries)
   - Global

4. **Current state** (optional but recommended):
   - Existing KOL relationships and engagement history
   - Current MSL team size and coverage
   - Known KOLs already engaged
   - Budget range for KOL activities

5. **Timeline** — planning horizon (e.g., "next 12 months", "pre-launch 18-month plan")

## KOL Strategy Structure

### 1. Strategic Context
- Why KOL engagement matters for this therapeutic area and product
- Current treatment landscape and where KOL influence is highest
- Competitive KOL engagement landscape (who are competitors engaging?)
- Strategic gaps this KOL plan aims to fill

### 2. KOL Identification

If ~~clinical trials data is connected, search for:
- Principal investigators on relevant clinical trials
- Authors of recent high-impact publications in the therapeutic area
- Presenters at major congresses in the last 2-3 years

Provide an initial KOL long list with key identifying information:

| Name | Institution | Specialty | Key Qualifications | Estimated Tier |
|------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|---------------|

### 3. KOL Tiering

Apply the KOL tier framework to segment the long list:

**Tier 1 — National/Global KOLs:**
- Selection criteria for this specific therapeutic area
- Recommended engagement activities
- Expected number: [suggest based on market size]

**Tier 2 — Regional KOLs:**
- Selection criteria
- Recommended engagement activities
- Expected number

**Tier 3 — Local/Community KOLs:**
- Selection criteria
- Recommended engagement activities
- Expected number

**Emerging KOLs:**
- Identification criteria (rising stars, early-career leaders)
- Recommended nurturing activities
- Expected number

### 4. Engagement Plan by Tier

For each tier, define the engagement model:

| Activity | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 | Emerging |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|----------|
| Advisory boards | Yes — national | Yes — regional | No | Occasional |
| Speaker programs | Yes — national/international | Yes — regional | Yes — local | Mentored |
| 1:1 MSL meetings | Quarterly | Semi-annual | Annual | As needed |
| Congress engagement | Priority meetings | Scheduled meetings | Booth visits | Introductions |
| Publication collaboration | Lead or senior author | Contributing author | Reviewer | Co-author |
| Clinical trial roles | Steering committee, PI | PI, sub-investigator | Site investigator | Fellow |
| Consulting | Strategic consulting | Tactical consulting | Occasional | Rare |

### 5. Advisory Board Plan

If the strategy includes advisory boards, outline:
- Number and type of advisory boards planned
- Proposed topics and scientific questions
- Suggested composition (specialty mix, geographic balance, tier mix)
- Timeline and format (in-person, virtual, hybrid)
- Budget estimate per advisory board
- Compliance requirements

### 6. Speaker Program Plan

If the strategy includes speaker programs, outline:
- National vs. regional speaker program structure
- Speaker identification and training plan
- Approved presentation topics
- Number of programs planned per quarter
- Target audience for each program type
- Compliance and transparency reporting requirements

### 7. MSL Deployment Alignment

Recommend how to align MSL territory coverage with the KOL map:
- KOL density by geography
- MSL-to-KOL ratio recommendations
- Territory adjustments needed
- Interaction frequency targets by tier
- Insight capture and reporting processes

### 9. Measurement Framework

| Metric | Definition | Target | Measurement Frequency |
|--------|-----------|--------|----------------------|
| KOL coverage | % of Tier 1/2 KOLs with at least one interaction | >90% Tier 1; >70% Tier 2 | Quarterly |
| Interaction quality | Internal rating of engagement depth | Trend improvement | Quarterly |
| Advisory board utilization | % of insights acted upon | >80% | Per advisory board |
| Speaker program reach | HCPs attending speaker programs | Set per quarter | Monthly |
| Publication output | Co-authored publications submitted/accepted | Set annually | Semi-annually |
| Sentiment tracking | KOL sentiment toward product/company | Neutral to positive trend | Semi-annually |
| Competitive share of KOL voice | Your KOL endorsements vs. competitor | Track trend | Annually |

### 10. Budget Framework

| Activity | Estimated Annual Cost | Notes |
|----------|----------------------|-------|
| Advisory boards | | FMV rates x number of advisors x meetings |
| Speaker programs | | Speaker fees + venue + logistics |
| MSL team (if expansion needed) | | Salary + travel + training |
| Congress engagement | | Meetings, dinners, logistics |
| Publication support | | Medical writing, journal fees |
| Consulting engagements | | FMV rates x hours |
| **Total** | | |

### 11. Compliance and Risk Mitigation
- Fair market value documentation for all KOL compensation
- Transparency reporting obligations (Sunshine Act, EFPIA)
- Separation of promotional and scientific activities
- Documentation requirements for all KOL interactions
- Conflict of interest management for KOLs involved in multiple activities
- Anti-kickback statute considerations for US engagements
